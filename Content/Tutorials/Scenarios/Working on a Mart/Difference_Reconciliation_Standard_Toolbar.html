Difference Reconciliation Standard Toolbar

The following lists the buttons that you can use in the Difference Reconciliation Standard toolbar and describes the purpose for each button.

Copy Item(s) to the left

Moves any items that do not match, from the right model to the left model.

Copy Item(s) to the right

Moves any items that do not match, from the left model to the right model.

Automerge nonconflicting differences

Automatically moves any items to the model that do not conflict.

Undo

Undoes the last ToRight, ToLeft, Match, or UnMatch operation.

Redo

Redoes a previous undo operation.

Help

Opens online help for the Difference Reconciliation dialog
