Open a Complete Compare Session

You can open a Complete Compare session previously saved as a CCS (Complete Compare Session) file. This feature allows you to quickly repeat a complete compare session, including all the customizations available in the Complete Compare Wizard panes.

To open a Complete Compare session

  1. Click Complete Compare on the Actions menu.

    The Complete Compare wizard opens.

  2. Click Load Session.

    The Open dialog opens.

  3. In the Open dialog, select a file with the *.CCS (Complete Compare Session) extension and click Open.

    The Complete Compare wizard is populated with all the selections in your previous Complete Compare session saved to the CCS file. You can make additional choices in the Complete Compare wizard, or click Compare to start the compare process.
