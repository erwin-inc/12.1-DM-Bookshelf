Enter a Definition for a Relationship

Use the options in the Definition tab in the Relationships editor to enter a definition for a relationship in a logical model. The definition that you enter should assist any person who reads the model in understanding the purpose for the relationship. Follow your organization's standards and conventions for defining the relationships in a model.

To enter a definition for a relationship

  1. Click Relationships on the Model menu.

    The Relationships editor opens.

  2. Select a relationship from the Relationship drop-down list for which you want to enter a definition, then click the Definition tab.

    The Definition tab opens.

  3. Enter the definition for the relationship and click OK.

    The relationship definition is saved and the Relationships editor closes.
