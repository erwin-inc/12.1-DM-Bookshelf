Resize Entities, Tables, and Views

You can resize entities, tables, or views on the diagram window so that they are smaller or larger.

To resize entities, tables, and views

  1. Select the entity, table, or view you want to resize so that the sizing handles are displayed and then move the cursor over a sizing handle.

    The cursor shape changes to indicate that you can adjust the size.

  2. Drag the sizing handle until the entity, table, or view is the size you want.

    The entity, table, or view is resized.

When you resize an entity, table, or view, any text in an attribute or column that extends beyond the height and width you specify does not display in the box. You must resize the box or reset automatic sizing to display all attribute and column text.
