Roll Down a Subtype Relationship with a Transformation

You can roll down a subtype relationship with a transformation.

To roll down a subtype relationship with a transformation

  1. Select the subtype relationship symbol .

    The Supertype-Subtype RollDown icon becomes active on the Transformations toolbar.

  2. Click the Supertype-Subtype RollDown icon.

    The wizard opens.

  3. Follow the instructions in the Supertype Subtype Roll-Down wizard. Click Finish.

    A subtype entity that corresponds to each source subtype entity is created.
