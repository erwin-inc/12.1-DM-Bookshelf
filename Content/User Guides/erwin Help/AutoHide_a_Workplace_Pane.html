Auto Hide a Workplace Pane

Use the Auto Hide option to hide a selected pane as a tab on the edge of the main workplace window. This feature is slightly different than the View, Windows option, in which you can select to remove the display of a pane from the workplace.

To auto hide a workplace pane

  1. Click the pin icon on the edge of the pane that you want to hide.

    The pane appears as a tab at the corresponding edge of the main window and the pin tilts to indicate that the pane is not pinned any more.

  2. Click the pin again to restore the pane.
