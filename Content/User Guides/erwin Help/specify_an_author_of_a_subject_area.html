Specify an Author of a Subject Area

You use the General tab in the Subject Area Editor to identify the author of a subject area.

To specify an author of a subject area

  1. On the ribbon, click Model > Subject Area Editor.

    The Subject Area Editor opens.

  2. Click the General tab.
  3. Select the subject area from the Subject Area list for which you want to specify an author name, enter the author's name in the Author box, and then click Close.

    The author name information is saved, and the Subject Area Editor closes.
