Display or Hide the Advisories Pane

By default, the Advisories pane is displayed in your workspace. You can hide the Advisories pane if you do not want to see the messages associated with the actions you perform.

To hide the Advisories pane, select the Advisories pane from the View menu. You can also close the pane by clicking on the close button in the upper right corner of the pane, or right-clicking on the pane and selecting Hide from the context menu.

To reverse this action, and display the pane again, select Advisories Pane from the View menu.
