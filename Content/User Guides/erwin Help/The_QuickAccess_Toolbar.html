The Quick Access Toolbar

The Quick Access Toolbar is located at the top-left corner of the workspace. It contains a group of shortcut buttons for common tasks, such as creating a new model, save a model, finding and replacing elements, and so on.

To add or remove shortcuts from the Quick Access Toolbar:

  1. Click the Customize Quick Access Toolbar arrow.
  2. Click More Commands. The Customize dialog box is displayed.
  3. In the list on the left-hand side, select the required task and click Add.
  4. Repeat the above step till you have added all the required tasks.
  5. Click Close.
