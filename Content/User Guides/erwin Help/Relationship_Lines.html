Relationship Lines

When you move an entity or add a relationship, the relationship line automatically adjusts so that it bends around other objects on the diagram window. The section of line between each bend in a relationship line is called a segment. A one-segment or two-segment line is drawn automatically unless the entities or tables sharing the relationship are closely aligned either horizontally or vertically.

Relationship lines on the diagram window position automatically when you add a line and when you move the source or destination of the relationship line. For example, when you move an entity, table, or view that has a relationship line attached to it, the position of the relationship line adjusts automatically to connect to the object in its new location.
