Create a Subject Area

You use the Subject Area Editor to create subject areas in the current model.

To create a subject area

  1. Click Subject Areas on the Model menu.

    You can also right-click Subject Areas in the Model Explorer and select New.

    The Subject Area Editor opens.

  2. Click the New icon.
  3. Enter a name for the new subject area.
  4. To include objects automatically, select the Auto-populate check box.

    All the objects of the model appear in the new subject area.

  5. Click Close.

    The subject area is saved and the Subject Area Editor closes.
