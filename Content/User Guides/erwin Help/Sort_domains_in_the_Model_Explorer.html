Sort Domains in the Model Explorer

You can sort domains in the Model Explorer from the Model view.

To sort domains in the Model Explorer

  1. Right-click Domains in the Model Explorer.

    A context sensitive menu opens.

  2. Click Sort Alphabetically to sort the domains in alphabetical order, or Sort Hierarchically to sort the domains in order of inheritance hierarchy.

    The domains are sorted.
