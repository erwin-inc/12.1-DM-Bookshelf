Move a Toolbar

When you position a toolbar next to the workspace boundary, it automatically positions, or "docks" along the edge of the workspace.

A floating toolbar is not positioned (docked) next to the workspace boundary. You can position and resize a floating toolbar anywhere on the workspace.

To move a toolbar, click the docked toolbar indicator () or click the title bar for the toolbar that you want to move, and drag the toolbar to the location where you want to position it.
