Auto Hide Feature

You can choose to use the auto hide feature for the Bulk Editor so that the Bulk Editor only appears when you need to work in it. To activate auto hide, click the pushpin in the Bulk Editor title bar. The Bulk Editor pane is now a tab in the application:

When you need to work in the Bulk Editor, hover your mouse over the Bulk Editor tab and the Bulk Editor pane is temporarily restored. You can now navigate and work in the Bulk Editor. When your mouse leaves the Bulk Editor area, it is auto-hidden once again.

To turn off auto hide, mouse over the Bulk Editor tab to restore the Bulk Editor pane, and then click the pushpin .
